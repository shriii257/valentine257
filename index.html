From the Heart…

Good Friday is here again… and it pulls me right back to 2022 — the day I told you what my heart had carried for so long. That memory still breathes quietly inside me. It was special… you were special. Maybe you still are, more than words can explain. I know I shattered something beautiful. What happened later — that one wrong step, that one blind moment — it haunts me in ways I can’t even describe. It wasn’t love. It wasn’t even intention. But it was a mistake… and it cost me someone irreplaceable. There’s no excuse — just a quiet ache, and a heart that still flinches at your name. I don’t expect anything from you. I just want you to know… not a single day passes without a thought of you, without a silent prayer hoping you’re okay… smiling, healing, even if I’m not a part of that anymore. On this Good Friday, I’m not asking for forgiveness… just hoping this message finds a little space in your heart. Even as a memory, even as a tear. Be safe. Be happy. And if ever, your heart feels heavy, know someone still carries a little weight for you too.
